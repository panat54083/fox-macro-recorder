# Privacy Policy for Fox Macro Recorder

**Last Updated:** December 25, 2024

## Overview

Fox Macro Recorder is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension handles user data.

## Data Collection

### What Data We Collect

Fox Macro Recorder collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

1. **Click Recording Data:**
   - Mouse click coordinates (x, y positions)
   - Timestamps of clicks
   - Element selectors where clicks occurred
   - Macro names you create

### What We DO NOT Collect

- Personal identifying information
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Passwords or sensitive form data
- Analytics or usage statistics
- Any data from websites you visit

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only:** All recorded macros are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not store, sync, or backup your data to any external servers
- **No Transmission:** Your data never leaves your browser
- **User Control:** You have complete control to delete your macros at any time

## Data Usage

The data collected is used solely for:
- Saving your recorded click sequences as macros
- Playing back saved macros when you request it
- Managing your macro library within the extension

We do not:
- Analyze your data
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Transmit your data over the network

## Permissions Explained

### Storage Permission
Required to save your recorded macros locally in your browser. Without this permission, the extension cannot function.

### Content Script Access (all_urls)
Required to record and replay clicks on any website you choose. The content script:
- Only runs on pages you actively visit
- Only activates when you explicitly open the recording panel
- Does not collect or transmit any website data
- Is hidden by default and user-controlled

## Third-Party Services

Fox Macro Recorder does not:
- Use any third-party analytics services
- Connect to any external APIs
- Include any tracking codes
- Load any external scripts or resources

## Data Security

- All data remains on your local device
- No network transmission means no interception risk
- Your macros are as secure as your browser's local storage
- You can export/import macros as JSON files for personal backup

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your saved macros within the extension
- **Delete:** Remove any or all macros at any time
- **Export:** Download your macros as JSON files
- **Control:** The extension is entirely under your control

## Children's Privacy

Fox Macro Recorder does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The extension is designed for general productivity use and does not target children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Open the Fox Macro Recorder panel
2. Click "Delete" on individual macros, or
3. Uninstall the extension to remove all data

Your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
